Translation
In treating epidemic febrile disorders, we often employ the method of clearing and reducing diaphragm heat to alleviate the heat in the upper jiao, while using collateral needling techniques to promote local qi and blood circulation. For cases of persistent lochia discharge postpartum, combining formulas that promote digestion and unblock stagnation with the adjustment of heel pain can effectively improve the patient's overall condition. For patients with the Ying nutrients phase pattern and the yin and yang deficiency pattern, we need to more meticulously adjust the medicinal formulas, focusing on nourishing and replenishing yin and yang, regulating and harmonizing qi and blood.
